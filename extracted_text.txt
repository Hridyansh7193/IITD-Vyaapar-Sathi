MSME AI Growth Platform – Master Project Documentation
1. Product Overview
The MSME AI Growth Platform is a SaaS system designed to help small and medium businesses analyze their sales data and receive AI-powered business insights.
The platform allows business owners to upload their sales datasets and automatically generates:• Sales analytics dashboards• Product performance insights• AI marketing recommendations• Demand forecasting• GST compliance summaries
The goal of the platform is to function as an AI-powered business advisor for MSMEs.
2. Problem Statement
Millions of MSMEs operate without access to advanced analytics tools. Business owners frequently rely on intuition instead of data-driven decision making.
Key challenges include:• Lack of sales analytics• Difficulty identifying high-performing products• Limited marketing insights• Complex GST compliance
The MSME AI Growth Platform provides an accessible analytics and AI system that simplifies these processes.
3. Product Vision
To build an AI-powered growth intelligence platform that enables small businesses to make informed decisions using automated analytics and recommendations.
4. Target Users
Primary users include:• Retail shop owners• Restaurant operators• Small distributors• Local business operators
These users typically have limited technical expertise and require simple dashboards with actionable insights.
5. User Journey
1. User registers or logs in.2. User uploads sales dataset.3. System validates and processes dataset.4. Analytics dashboard is generated.5. AI insights and recommendations appear.6. User reviews insights and downloads reports.
6. System Architecture
Frontend Layer:React / Next.js interface for dashboards and analytics pages.
Backend Layer:FastAPI server handling APIs and business logic.
Data Processing Layer:Python + Pandas for data analysis and aggregation.
AI Layer:OpenAI / Gemini integration for insight generation.
Database Layer:MongoDB for storing user data, datasets, and analytics results.

10. Functional Requirements
FR1: Users must be able to create accounts.FR2: Users must be able to upload CSV sales datasets.FR3: System must validate uploaded data.FR4: Analytics dashboards must be generated automatically.FR5: AI engine must provide business insights.FR6: System must estimate GST obligations.FR7: Reports must be downloadable.FR8: AI assistant must respond to business queries.
11. Non-Functional Requirements
Performance:Analytics dashboards should load within 5 seconds.
Security:Secure authentication and protected data storage.
Scalability:System should support large datasets and multiple users.
Usability:User interface must be simple and intuitive.
